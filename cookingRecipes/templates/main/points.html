What is the points system?

The points system rewards a user for interacting with the website. Every time you create a recipe, add a picture, or otherwise contribute to the website, you get points!

  • Creating a recipe: +10
  • Adding a rating: +5
  • Submitting a picture: +5

You can check your point total (and your rank) on your profile. Remember:

  1. Rookie: 0 - 99 points
  2. Contributer: 100 - 499 points
  3. Legend: 500+ points

Leveling up your rank allows you to access more exciting features within the cooking recipe website! While all users have access to the basic features, such as recipe creation and browsing, Contributers have the ability to add bookmarks, making accessing your favorite recipes easier than ever! On top of that, Legends have the unique chance to change color schemes, search by ingredient, and other amazing perks!
