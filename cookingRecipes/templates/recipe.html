Potatoes

Yummy rustic poatoes for any occasion, made with a whole bunch of delicious stuff

Total time: 60 minutes



Ingredients

  1. 10 Potatoes
  2. 1/2 tsp Salt
  3. 1/2 tsp Pepper
  4. 2 bunches Rosemary
  5. 2 tsp Olive Oil
  6. 3 cloves Garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Wash potatoes and dice them to be small.
  3. Mince the garlic and mix into the potatoes with the salt and pepper.
  4. Add the rosemary and the olive oil and mix thourouly.
  5. Put the potatoes in the oven and bake for 40 minutes
